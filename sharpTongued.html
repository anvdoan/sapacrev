Sharp-Tongued

Noe Conahan

Dimensions: 12x9 in, Materials: oil paint on canvas, ink on origami paper. This painting was inspired by the formative and often unfair things women hear growing up. The lips are intended to illustrate the mouth of society, and the surrounding phrases on the colored paper are meant to bring to life some examples of the narrative created for young girls
