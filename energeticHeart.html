Energetic Heart

Nick Ringe

Original is 9"x12", alcohol marker and pen.

This piece means a lot to me, I struggle with words but here's what I can say - every element was intentional, and has a deeper meaning that I invite the viewer to interpret on their own. I made this drawing early in January of this year, and I intend for it to be interactive.

Here are the simple instructions: Gratitude, Compassion, Humility, Forgiveness, Understanding, Valor (courage to speak Truth to injustice) - pick a specific time in your life when someone who loved you expressed one of these feelings towards you and try to really *feel* that expression in your Heart. Hold it there for a couple moments, and then release that same feeling towards something or someone who's wronged you in the past, then let it go.

This drawing can be used to aid your imagination to experience the 'unseen' effects of the above Heart-centered behaviors...how they move interact within and outside of the limits of time and space.

I've known it loosely as The Energetic Heart, and it has helped me personally beyond anything I can say or make sense to the mind. My hope is to give at least one other person a version of this experience that was shared with me. Thanks so much for your time.
